About Me

I'm Gustavo Aguirre, a Full Stack Developer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. Currently pursuing my degree at UNLP faculty while continuously expanding my skillset through practical projects and online courses.

My development journey focuses on the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) with a strong emphasis on TypeScript for type-safe applications. I'm passionate about building complete solutions that solve real problems, from designing databases and creating robust APIs to crafting intuitive user interfaces.

Beyond coding, I'm committed to writing clean, maintainable code following industry best practices, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies and tackle challenging projects. I'm currently available for freelance opportunities and excited to collaborate on meaningful projects.
